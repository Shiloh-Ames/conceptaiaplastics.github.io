Company History

2005

September 2005 marked another expansion for AIA Plastics, Inc. After giving up the lease on the 12,000 sq. ft. building, AIA purchased a 30,000 sq. ft. building for our plastics divisions fabrication needs. This acquisition gives us 50,000 square feet of m.


AIA Plastics, Inc. purchases 30,000 sq. ft. building

2003

2003 was a great year for growth for AIA Plastics, Inc. So much in fact that AIA was forced to lease a 12,000 sq. ft. building for fabrication.


AIA Plastics, Inc. leases another 12,000 sq. ft. building

1999

December of 1999 AIA Industries, which is the architectural skylight arm of AIA Plastics, Inc., split off as a separate corporation but is still closely associated with AIA Plastics.


AIA Industries becomes separate corporation

1998

Growth once again required AIA to add another 10,000 sq. ft. to our existing building.


Another expansion for AIA Plastics, Inc.

1983

In 1983 AIA Plastics, Inc. purchased a 10,000 sq. ft. building at our present location.


AIA Plastics, Inc. moved into a new building

1977

Jim Donaldson started the business with a 1700 sq. ft. building. Shortly thereafter, AIA Plastics, Inc. grew into a 5100 sq. ft. building.


AIA Plastics opens for business

Gain From Experience

Founded in 1977, A.I.A. Plastics, Inc. is a plastic fabrication company specializing in CNC routing, vacuum forming, thermo forming, welding, fabrication and machining of all types of plastics. A.I.A. Plastics, Inc. has the capability and capacity to:

  • Create through innovative design approaches.
  • Tooling for the simplest to the most complex task.
  • Setup to produce quality plastic parts.
  • Production guaranteed to get the job done.
  • Perform Assembly and finish operations on anything we create.

    • Plastics Distributor

    A.I.A. Plastics, Inc., is also a full-line plastics distributor, stocking and selling all types of plastic sheet, rod, and tubing to industrial and architectural accounts. We are conveniently located in a modern 50,000 square foot facility and employ over 60 people to serve your plastics needs.
